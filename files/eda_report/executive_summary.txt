PURPOSE
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Data-driven analysis of customer churn at a European bank. 10,000 customers
across France, Spain, and Germany. Focus: behavioural engagement and product
usage — not demographics — as the primary drivers of retention.

SCALE OF THE CHALLENGE
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • 2,037 customers (20.37%) churned — nearly 1 in 5.
  • 4,849 customers (48.5%) are currently inactive.
  • 3,617 customers (36.2%) hold zero balance.
  • 621 premium customers are disengaged — highest silent churn risk.

KEY FINDINGS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  1. ENGAGEMENT RETENTION RATIO: 1.88×
     Active churn: 14.27%  |  Inactive churn: 26.85%

  2. THE 3-PRODUCT CLIFF
     2-product churn: 7.58% (optimal)  |  3+: 85.89% (critical failure)

  3. GERMANY REGIONAL RISK
     Germany: 32.44%
     Spain: 16.67%
     France: 16.15%

  4. GENDER CHURN GAP
     Female: 25.07%
     Male: 16.46%

  5. CREDIT CARD STICKINESS: 0.63 pp
     Card holders: 20.18%  |  No card: 20.81%

  6. HIGH-BALANCE DISENGAGEMENT: 49.88%
     49.88% of premium customers inactive. Balance ≠ loyalty.

  7. STICKY RETENTION ADVANTAGE: 17.63 pp
     Sticky: 5.05%  |  Non-Sticky: 22.68%

STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  P1 — INACTIVE RE-ENGAGEMENT [IMMEDIATE]
       Target 4,849 inactive members. Trigger: 90+ day inactivity.

  P2 — 3-PRODUCT BUNDLE AUDIT [URGENT]
       3+ products = 85.89% churn. Audit and redesign all bundles.

  P3 — PREMIUM SILENT CHURN PROGRAMME [HIGH VALUE]
       Assign relationship managers to 621 at-risk premium customers.

  P4 — GERMANY REGIONAL PROGRAMME [STRATEGIC]
       Germany at 32.44% churn. Local product audit + dedicated support.

  P5 — GENDER-RESPONSIVE ENGAGEMENT [SCALABLE]
       Female churn 25.07% vs male 16.46%. Targeted campaigns needed.

  P6 — CREDIT CARD ACQUISITION PUSH [LONG-TERM]
       Only 71% hold a card. Offer first-year fee waivers.

  P7 — RSI CRM INTEGRATION [SYSTEMIC]
       Flag RSI < 30 for monthly automated outreach.

CONCLUSION
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Engagement and product alignment — not balance — drive retention.
Three priorities demand immediate action: inactivity gap, 3-product cliff,
and Germany regional risk. The KPI framework is ready for CRM integration.
